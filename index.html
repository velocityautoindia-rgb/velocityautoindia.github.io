21+ Years of Automotive Excellence

Velocity Auto India was founded on a singular principle: ultra-luxury automobile owners deserve direct access to genuine OEM parts at transparent pricing—without compromising on authenticity or expertise.

Our founders, Chris and Giannis, spent two decades in high-end automotive logistics and parts distribution. Their vision was simple: bridge the gap between India's growing luxury collector base and the world's best OEM suppliers.

Today, we maintain active partnerships with authorized distributors across the UK, EU, and North America. Every part we supply carries full manufacturer authentication. Our warehouse in Bangalore stocks over 5,000 SKUs across all six marques we serve.

We've earned the trust of India's ultra-high-net-worth individuals by respecting their time, protecting their investments, and delivering on every promise.
